Biography of Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah

  • Born on December 25, 1876, in Karachi.
  • Studied law in London and became a barrister.
  • Joined Indian National Congress but later became a strong advocate for Muslim rights.
  • Played a key role in founding the All-India Muslim League.
  • Led the Pakistan Movement with his powerful vision and leadership.
  • Successfully achieved the creation of Pakistan on August 14, 1947.
  • Became the first Governor-General of Pakistan.
  • Passed away on September 11, 1948, but his legacy lives on.
